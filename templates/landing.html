📊 Dashboard

Your dashboard See account performance without digging through clutter

📈 Analytics

See where you perform best Break down performance by session, symbol, and behavior

The analytics view surfaces win rate, session timing, pair performance, and other account-specific patterns so review is based on real behavior instead of guesswork.

Use it to isolate where performance actually comes from and where recurring mistakes keep showing up before they become habits.

About the creator

A real early-stage product, built from an actual problem.

My name is Daniel. I am an A-Level graduate from Singapore, currently learning computer science to prepare for university, and I am also a aspiring forex trader.

I built FX Journal because the other journaling tools I found either felt too expensive, too cluttered, or too old. I wanted something simpler that made journaling realistic to keep up with.

It is shaped from the perspective of someone actively reviewing and learning from trades, so the product stays grounded in the kind of workflow traders actually need day to day.

This project is for traders who value simplicity and do not want a lot of overhead just to review their own trades.
